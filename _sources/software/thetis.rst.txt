Thetis
######

`Thetis <https://thetisproject.org>`__ is a Python-based unstructured mesh
discontinuous Galerkin coastal ocean model built upon `Firedrake
<firedrake.html>`__.

I was a core member of the Thetis development team during my time as a PhD
student and postdoc at `Imperial College London <https://imperial.ac.uk>`__.
