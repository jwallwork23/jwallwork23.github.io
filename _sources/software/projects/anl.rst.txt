Automatic differentiation in PETSc
==================================

Integration of the `ADOL-C <https://github.com/coin-or/ADOL-C>`__ automatic
differentiation tool into `PETSc <https://www.mcs.anl.gov/petsc>`__ numerical
solver library. PETSc is written in C, while ADOL-C is written in C++.
