nextSIM-DG
==========

`nextSIM-DG <https://github.com/nextsimhub/nextsimdg>`__ is a next-generation
discontinuous Galerkin sea ice model written in C++.

I was actively involved in nextSIM-DG development during a project as part of my
work as a Research Software Engineer at the `Institute of Computing for Climate
Science (ICCS) <https://iccs.cam.ac.uk>`__.
